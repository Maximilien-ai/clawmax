# Weekly Status Report

## Objective
Generate a comprehensive weekly status report for leadership review.

## Instructions

### 1. Week Summary (Feb 31 - Mar 6, 2026)
- **Major Accomplishments**: List 3-5 key achievements this week
- **Completed Tasks**: Summarize completed work items
- **Metrics**: Provide relevant metrics (PRs merged, issues closed, etc.)

### 2. Next Week's Plan (Mar 7-13, 2026)
- **Top Priorities**: List 3-5 priorities for next week
- **Planned Deliverables**: What will be shipped or completed?
- **Key Milestones**: Any important dates or deadlines?

### 3. Risks & Blockers
- **Current Blockers**: What's preventing progress?
- **Upcoming Risks**: What might become a problem?
- **Mitigation Plans**: How are you addressing these?

### 4. Resource Needs
- **Help Needed**: What support do you need from leadership?
- **Dependencies**: What are you waiting on from others?

### 5. Team Health
- **Morale**: How is team energy and motivation?
- **Capacity**: Is the team over/under-utilized?
- **Concerns**: Any team dynamics or issues to address?

## Output Format
Provide a structured report suitable for leadership review.
