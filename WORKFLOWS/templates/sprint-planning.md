# Sprint Planning Workflow

## Objective
Prepare for sprint planning meeting by reviewing backlog, capacity, and priorities.

## Instructions

### 1. Sprint Review (Previous Sprint)
- **Completed**: What was delivered last sprint?
- **Incomplete**: What didn't get done and why?
- **Velocity**: How many story points were completed?
- **Lessons Learned**: What went well? What could improve?

### 2. Capacity Planning (Upcoming Sprint)
- **Team Availability**: Who is available for the full sprint?
- **Time Off**: Any planned PTO or holidays?
- **Other Commitments**: Meetings, interviews, support rotation?
- **Estimated Capacity**: How many story points can the team commit to?

### 3. Backlog Review
- **Top Priorities**: What are the highest priority items?
- **Dependencies**: Any blockers or dependencies to resolve?
- **Ready for Development**: Which stories are fully defined?
- **Technical Debt**: Should we allocate capacity for tech debt?

### 4. Sprint Goal Proposal
- **Theme**: What's the overarching goal for this sprint?
- **Success Criteria**: How will we measure success?
- **Key Deliverables**: What MUST ship this sprint?

### 5. Risks & Concerns
- **Scope Risks**: Is the proposed scope achievable?
- **Technical Risks**: Any unknowns or complex tasks?
- **Resource Risks**: Any capacity constraints?

## Output Format
Provide a structured proposal for the sprint planning meeting including:
- Recommended sprint goal
- Proposed story list with priorities
- Capacity analysis
- Risk mitigation plan
