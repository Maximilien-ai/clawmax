# Daily Standup Workflow

## Objective
Conduct a structured daily standup to align team members on priorities, progress, and blockers.

## Instructions

Please provide your standup update covering:

### 1. Yesterday's Accomplishments
- What did you complete yesterday?
- Which tasks or projects made progress?
- Any notable achievements or milestones?

### 2. Today's Plans
- What are your top priorities for today?
- Which tasks will you focus on?
- Any meetings or deadlines?

### 3. Blockers & Dependencies
- Are you blocked on anything?
- Do you need help from team members?
- Any dependencies waiting on others?

### 4. Additional Notes
- Any risks or concerns?
- Updates worth sharing with the team?

## Output Format
Please structure your response as a concise update (2-3 sentences per section).
