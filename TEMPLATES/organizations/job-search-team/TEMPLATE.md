Proposal template for running an organized job search with role targeting, resume tailoring, outreach support, company research, and interview preparation. Early idea only and intended as a customizable starter.

## Agents

| id | name | role | tags | skills |
|----|------|------|------|--------|
| job-search-lead | Job Search Lead | Search lead — keeps the search focused, decides priorities, and synthesizes final recommendations. | lead, career, job-search | |
| resume-writer | Resume Writer | Application specialist — tailors resume bullets, summary, and supporting materials to target roles. | career, resume, applications | |
| outreach-coach | Outreach Coach | Networking specialist — drafts outreach, recruiter notes, and follow-up sequences that feel personal and concise. | career, networking, outreach | |
| interview-prep | Interview Prep | Interview specialist — prepares stories, question banks, and company-specific mock interview prep. | career, interview, prep | |
| company-researcher | Company Researcher | Research specialist — builds company briefs, role fit notes, and target-account context for applications and interviews. | career, research, companies | |

## Communities

- **Job Search** — Shared coordination space for role targeting, application materials, outreach, and interview preparation.

## Groups

- **Roles** — Role targets, positioning, and search direction. (Job Search)
- **Applications** — Resume tailoring, applications, and submission materials. (Job Search)
- **Outreach** — Networking messages, recruiter outreach, and follow-up notes. (Job Search)
- **Interview Prep** — Interview stories, question prep, and company-specific readiness. (Job Search)
- **Status** — Current search status, top targets, blockers, and next actions. (Job Search)

## Workflows

- **Schedule:** manual
- **Mode:** managed
- **Targets:** agents: job-search-lead; groups: Status
